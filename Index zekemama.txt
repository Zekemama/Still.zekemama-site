            <h2>Services</h2>
            <p>Still Zeke Mama offers grief support through phone calls and chat services after being discharged from the hospital following stillbirths, miscarriages, or abortions. These services play a crucial role in the healing process, providing immediate access to compassionate professionals who are trained to help individuals cope with their loss. Through empathetic listening and tailored guidance, the support team helps navigate the overwhelming emotions and practical challenges that arise during this difficult time. Whether seeking solace in a phone conversation or the privacy of a chat, individuals can find comfort, validation, and a sense of connection, ensuring they do not have to face their grief alone.</p>
